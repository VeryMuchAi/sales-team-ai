# Factorial - Prospect Research Profile

## 1. Company Overview

**Company Name:** Factorial (formerly Factorial HR)
**Website:** https://factorialhr.com
**Headquarters:** Barcelona, Spain
**Founded:** 2016
**Industry:** HR Technology / SaaS
**Valuation:** $1B+ (Unicorn since October 2022)

Factorial is a cloud-based business management software platform designed to streamline HR processes for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). The platform integrates time management, talent management, payroll, finance, and IT management into a single solution. Their tagline is "The business software to manage your whole team," and they emphasize putting "the human back in HR" by automating repetitive administrative tasks.

**Key Product Modules:**
- HRIS (document management, employee portal, e-signatures)
- Time Management (time tracking, shift scheduling, time-off requests)
- Talent Management (performance reviews, recruitment, onboarding, goal tracking)
- Payroll & Finance (payroll automation, expense management, financial reporting)
- IT Management (device management, SaaS oversight)
- Factorial One (AI agent for report generation and decision-making support)

**Target Market:** SMEs across industries including retail, technology, healthcare, manufacturing, and construction. Serves over 15,000 companies across 65+ countries.

**Pricing:** Modular pricing starting at $8 per employee per month.
