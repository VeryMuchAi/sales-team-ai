# Platzi - Prospect Research Profile

## 1. Company Overview

**Company Name:** Platzi
**Website:** https://platzi.com
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA, USA (legal HQ) / Bogota, Colombia (operational center)
**Founded:** 2011 (as Mejorando la Web), formally incorporated 2014
**Industry:** EdTech / Online Education / SaaS
**Stage:** Series B

Platzi is the largest online technology education platform in Latin America, serving over 6 million students across the Spanish-speaking world. The platform offers 1,700+ courses organized into 17 specialty schools covering programming, AI/data science, design, marketing, business, fintech, cybersecurity, leadership, and more.

Platzi positions itself as "the technology school of Latin America," providing structured learning pathways that guide users from beginner to professional levels. The platform combines video courses, interactive testing, AI-powered tutoring, community support, and professional certifications.

**Key Product Areas:**
- 17 learning schools (web development, AI/data science, design, fintech, startups, etc.)
- Personalized learning routes
- Professional certifications (partnerships with ETS for TOEFL, CompTIA Security+, Google Cloud)
- AI-powered tutoring and assessments
- Offline content access
- Platzi Business (B2B enterprise training platform)

**Target Market:**
- Individual learners across Latin America (B2C)
- Enterprises seeking to upskill teams (B2B via Platzi Business)
- Career changers and professional development seekers
- Technology professionals specializing in development and engineering

**Pricing:** Freemium model with premium subscription tiers (monthly/annual). Platzi Business offers enterprise plans with custom pricing.

## 8. Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors:**
- **Coursera** - University-backed, 4,500+ courses, focus on formal education and degrees (generates significantly more revenue)
- **Udemy** - Self-paced, affordable, massive catalog (generates 13,166% more revenue than Platzi)
- **Coderhouse** - LATAM-focused coding bootcamp
- **Skillshare** - Creative and business skills
- **Udacity** - Tech nanodegrees (similar tech focus)

**Other Competitors:** Treehouse, Khan Academy, Domestika, LinkedIn Learning, edX

**Competitive Advantages:**
- **LATAM market dominance:** Largest platform in Spanish for technology education
- **Structured learning paths:** Organized progression from beginner to professional
- **Community-driven:** Active student community, Platzi Conf events, strong brand loyalty
- **B2B offering:** Platzi Business serves 4,000+ companies with team training
- **Industry-led content:** Courses taught by professionals from top tech companies
- **Local relevance:** Content contextualized for LATAM market needs and job market
- **Affordable pricing:** More accessible than US-based competitors for LATAM audiences

**Weaknesses:**
- Revenue significantly smaller than global competitors (Udemy, Coursera)
- No new funding round since 2021 may limit aggressive expansion
- Platform primarily Spanish-language, limiting global reach
